Take a ride if you dare on this coaster. Although it looks innocent enough, the screams from the top of the ride say otherwise! Eliciting screams from the passengers as the coaster seems to go off the track before whipping rapidly around. Sudden drops and bunny hops add to the thrills--it'll make you want to "Blast Off!”

Strap yourself in and experience the thrill of flying! Lay on your stomach and take off into the wild blue yonder as you are lifted high in the air and tilted forward giving way to the exhilarating sensation of free flight.

Brave the rapids and you're rewarded with a thrilling good time! Step into the raft with your friends and family and set off down the river, spinning and turning through rapids, water falls and even a whirlpool!

Are your kids bored? Fret not! Let them have a whale of a time at this never ending playground.
